About Us

Hello! We are the Photo Bros, Joe Bro and Moe Bro. We've been shooting photos since we could lift a camera! (Although perhaps not always as well as we do now.) It's always been our dream to open a photo studio. Joe has a previous successful career as a wedding photographer, while Moe worked as a freelance photographer for magazines. Now the Bros have combined their talents to create this friendly local studio dedicated to bringing unique quality photos to the people of Vancouver.
