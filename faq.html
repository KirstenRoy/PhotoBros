FAQ

  1. Do you ship worldwide?
  2. Can I commission a photograph?

  4. Where are you take your photographs?

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, certainly! Although there will be an extra fee for international shipping based on country.

Can I commission a photograph?

If there is a specific photo you are looking for, let us know and we'll let you know if that's something we can get for you. We'll certainly try! Unfortunately we cannot provide portrait photography services.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional DSLRs, but we've never quite agreed. One of us uses a Nikon and the other uses a Canon.

Where do you take your photographs?

The majority of our photographs are taken in Vancouver BC and the surrounding areas. Sometimes we go on trips to exotic locations, such as our trip to Morroco, and we always take our cameras with us!

Do you photograph private events?

No, only flying monkeys photograph events. Stop that, Moe, I told you not to type. Sorry, we regret to inform you that we cannot photograph events.
